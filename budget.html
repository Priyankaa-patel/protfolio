budgeting & saving app

Duration: Jul’22 - Aug’22 Role: concept to delivery

The FutureSafe is a mobile app and responsive website to help young adults in budgeting, saving money, and overcome unnecessary expenses. Where user can track their money, manage their expenses, and set goals to save money for that. The typical users are young adults.
