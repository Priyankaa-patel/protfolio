Website for fresh juice store

Duration: 10 weeks Role: concept to delivery

The Power Punchis an online fresh juice store. Where users can get their daily nutrition. They can check product details, benefits, and ingredients of each product and choose as per their need. The user also can place their daily or weekly order at one click and they get their juice delivered to their doorstep every day.
